BetterCallClaude — Swiss Legal Intelligence for Cowork Desktop

The Swiss law plugin for Claude. 20 agents, 24 commands, 12 skills, and 9 MCP servers covering federal and cantonal law across all 26 Swiss cantons in DE/FR/IT/EN.

Research BGE/ATF/DTF precedents against official sources. Draft contracts and court submissions with verified Swiss citations. Triage NDAs with Swiss-law criteria (GREEN/YELLOW/RED). Run three-agent adversarial analysis. Protect client confidentiality with built-in Art. 321 StGB privacy routing.

Customize with a local playbook for firm-specific positions, risk thresholds, and citation preferences. Compatible with Anthropic's Legal plugin — BetterCallClaude handles Swiss law, the official plugin handles US law.

No API keys required. MCP servers connect via HTTP.
